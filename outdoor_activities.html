Outdoor Activities in Hampton Roads

If you like being outside, then you've come to the right place. Hampton Roads has endless opportunities to be active outside. Whether you want to lounge at the beach, surf some waves, ride a bike, hike some trails, kayak, deep sea fish, or golf, you can do just about anything here.
Below you will find some activities you can do to have some fun in the sun or just get away from everything.

Norfolk

  • Learn to sail with SailTime or Sail Nauticus.
  • Run, walk, or bike on the 10.5 mile Elizabeth River Trail, which runs along the banks of downtown all the way through Old Dominion University. Click here to view and download a map of the trail.
  • Rent a Pedego electric bike and ride around town.
  • Go "mountain" biking along 2.2 miles of single track in Northside Park.
  • Go fishing off the Ocean View Fishing Pier.
  • Rent a kayak, jet ski, or a hydrobike!
  • Bring a chair and chill at Ocean View Beach all day!

Virginia Beach

  • Rent a bike a cruise along the boardwalk that runs along Virginia Beach!
  • Hit the links at Virginia National, a former TPC golf course that is now available for anyone to play.
  • Rent a Pedego electric bike and ride around town.
  • Pick your own strawberries at Flanagan Farms, Henley Farms, or Brookdale Farm. Or attend the Pungo Strawberry Festival.
  • Hike the trails at First Landing State Park or Back Bay National Wildlife Refuge.
  • Bring your camera and go birding at False Cape State Park or its nextdoor neighbor, Back Bay NWR.
  • Enjoy the relatively peaceful beaches in nearby Sandbridge, which give a nice distraction from the crowds at Oceanfront in Virginia Beach.

Williamsburg

  • Charter a sailboat with Williamsburg Charter Sails.
  • Rent a bike and ride all the way to Richmond along the 52 mile Capital Trail. Along the way, stop at the Historic Jamestowne Bike Trail.
  • Take a leisurely drive along the 23 mile Colonial Parkway.
  • Play a round at Colonial Heritage Golf Club.
  • Go fishing at Chickahominy Riverfront Park or launch a kayak at College Landing Park.
  • Rent a kayak at Bay Country Kayaking.
