Fun Family Activities in Hampton Roads

Sometimes the weather doesn't cooperate or you need more family-friendly activities that everyone in the family can enjoy.
Below are some activities the whole family can enjoy, both indoors and out.

Norfolk

  • Walk through the Norfolk Botanical Garden.
  • Visit all the animals at the Virginia Zoo.
  • Admire the art at the Chrysler Museum or watch a glass blowing demonstration at their glass blowing studio.
  • Learn about the stars at Old Dominion University's Pretlow Planetarium.
  • Catch a Norfolk Tides baseball game or a Norfolk Admirals hockey game.
  • To find more things to do, check out "Visit Norfolk."

Virginia Beach

  • Visit the Military Aviation Museum to learn about America's military aviation history.
  • Check out the Virginia Aquarium and learn about all the sea creatures that inhabit the Chesapeake Bay and Virginia Beach waters.
  • Take the kids to Ocean Breeze Water Park and let them expend some energy while you float along in the lazy river.
  • Go dolphin or whale watching with Rudee Tours or go kayaking with dolphins!
  • Walk up and down Atlantic Ave and browse all the beach shops.

Williamsburg

  • Stroll through the Williamsburg Botanical Garden.
  • Fly above the treeline at Go Ape! ziplining.
  • Relive life in 1600s America at Colonial Williamsburg and Jamestown Settlement.
  • Walk in the footsteps of fallen soldiers at Yorktown Battlefield.
  • For the thrill seekers in the family, check out Busch Gardens for an adrenaline rush!
  • Take a tour and set sail on the schooner Alliance in Yorktown's harbor.
